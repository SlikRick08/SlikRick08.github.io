Current Happenings in the World of Chess

Magnus Wins World Chess Championship

Magnus Carlsen defies the odds yet again, claiming his "World Chess Champion" title while defeating Fabiano Caruana in a 12-game match. The game came down to tiebreaks, but Magnus came out victorius.

Research Finds Chess More Beneficial In Lower Grades

The preliminary findings of a multi-year study across the state of Alabama seem to indicate that chess instruction may improve student performance, especially amongst younger students.

Google's AlphaZero Shows Promise

In news reminiscent of last year's shockwave, AlphaZero, Google's AI chess engine, defeated StockFish 8 in a new 1,000 game match, scoring +155 to StockFish's -6. AlphaZero continues to set new boundaries in the world of machine learning and chess.
